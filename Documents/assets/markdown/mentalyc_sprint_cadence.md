# Mentalyc Sprint Cadence

*Source: Mentalyc_Sprint_Cadence.docx*

## Sprint Cadence & Scrum Events


Sprint Duration: 1 week (fast iteration for early feedback).
Daily Scrum: 15 mins, async OK (e.g. Slack thread or Loom).
Sprint Planning: 1 hour (Monday AM).
Sprint Review: With design partners or proxy (Friday).
Sprint Retrospective: Internal + async feedback doc.
