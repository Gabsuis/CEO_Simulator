# Mentalyc Product Backlog

*Source: Mentalyc_Product_Backlog.docx*

## Initial Product Backlog

### EPIC 1: Capture & Transcription

- As a therapist, I want to record a session from mobile/desktop.

- As a therapist, I want accurate transcription (Whisper/GSTT) cleaned with filler removal.

### EPIC 2: Dual-Mode Notes (Progress + Process)

- As a therapist, I want to toggle between SOAP-style and narrative note formats.

- As a therapist, I want my preferred note format saved per patient.

### EPIC 3: Insight Extraction

- As a therapist, I want recurring themes/emotions auto-extracted from notes.

- As a parent, I want a short summary of my child’s therapy progress.

### EPIC 4: Security & Compliance

- As a user, I want my data encrypted (in transit + at rest).

- As a user, I want to explicitly approve data capture via a consent workflow.

### EPIC 5: Per-Patient Notebook

- As a therapist, I want notes to auto-attach to each patient’s notebook.

- As a therapist, I want to add free-text comments post-session.

### EPIC 6: Bilingual UX (HE/EN)

- As a Hebrew-speaking therapist, I want a localized UI.

- As a user, I want quick-insert goals/outcomes checkboxes.

### EPIC 7: Customization Engine

- As a therapist, I want to customize note output format (SOAP/narrative mix).

- As an org, I want templates enforced at department level.
